# 🧠 Assets and Risk Evaluation

## 📌 What is an Asset?

In cybersecurity, an asset is anything that has value to its owner.

Assets can be:

- 💻 Digital (accounts, data, software)
- 🏢 Physical (devices, servers)
- 📄 Information (personal or sensitive data)

---

## 🎯 Why Assets Matter

Not all assets are equally important.

👉 Some losses are minor  
👉 Some can have serious or long-term consequences  

Cybersecurity focuses on protecting the **most valuable assets first**

---

## ⚖️ Evaluating Risk Using CIA Triad

For each asset, we evaluate:

- Confidentiality (privacy)
- Integrity (correctness)
- Availability (access)

---

## 📊 Impact Scale

| Rating | Meaning |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Low impact (no real issue) |
| 3 | Medium impact (minor disruption) |
| 5 | High impact (serious/life-changing) |

---

## 🧪 Example Evaluations (New Examples)

### 📱 1. Mobile Phone

| Objective | Impact | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Confidentiality | 5 | Contains personal messages, apps, and accounts |
| Integrity | 4 | Modified data/apps could cause malfunction |
| Availability | 5 | Losing access disrupts communication and daily tasks |

---

### 🖼️ 2. Personal Photo Collection

| Objective | Impact | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Confidentiality | 3 | Some photos may be private |
| Integrity | 5 | Losing or altering memories is irreversible |
| Availability | 4 | Temporary loss is frustrating |

---

### 🌐 3. Social Media Account

| Objective | Impact | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Confidentiality | 3 | Personal chats and data exposure |
| Integrity | 4 | Fake posts can damage reputation |
| Availability | 3 | Inconvenient but recoverable |

---

### 💳 4. Online Banking Account

| Objective | Impact | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Confidentiality | 5 | Sensitive financial information |
| Integrity | 5 | Incorrect transactions = serious loss |
| Availability | 4 | Temporary loss can disrupt payments |

---

## 🔍 Key Observation

Different assets prioritize different objectives.

👉 Example:
- Banking → Integrity + Confidentiality  
- Photos → Integrity  
- Phone → All three  

---

## 🎯 Why This is Important

Security resources are limited.

👉 You must prioritize protection based on:

- Value of the asset  
- Impact of loss  

---

## 🛡️ Real-Life Decision Example

- You use a very strong password for your banking account  
- But a simpler password for a less critical app  

👉 This is risk-based decision making

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

In real systems, security is not about protecting everything equally, but about making smart trade-offs based on impact.

This idea is especially important in large systems where resources are limited and priorities must be clearly defined.
